Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply browse our courses, click on the course you're interested in, and hit the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be guided through the payment process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with a course, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes, upon completion of any course, you'll receive a certificate that you can share on LinkedIn or add to your resume.
